About US

A coalition of individuals, organizations and communities against bigotry and White Supremacy in the Pacific Coast.

Our Story

A coalition of individuals, organizations and communities against bigotry and White Supremacy in the Pacific Coast.

The objectives of the Coalition are to :

- Monitor and research all forms of bigotry in the Pacific Coast.

- Raise awareness and organize opposition to all forms of bigotry in the Pacific Coast.

- Oppose all laws that enforce bigotry across Canada and around the world.

Structure

Decisions will be made by consensus. Only indigenous and people of colour will able to withhold consensus.

Code of Conduct and Accountability :

- We will be held accountable and be transparent in all our communications.

- We will use constructive and respectful language in all communications.

- We will not make statements of fact nor will blame be assumed without knowing all facts. We will ask questions and not make assumptions.

- Everyone will have the right to be heard with respect without interruptions.

- We resolve all conflict in person. Not on-line.

- Constructive criticism can be accepted in full or part.
